Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Dhivyesh. I am currently a Year 12 student at Garden International School studying A-levels. I chose Further maths, Physics and Chemistry as my AS level subjects as I am keen in problem solving and finding answers to simple and often ignored questions. Although I do not take computer science, I have found that my passions lie in programming and building useful programs.

 This website, for example, is a testament to my creative and project based learning skills. The finished product, after many hours of grueling and seemingly directionless debugging, gives my work meaning and motivates me to push through and succeed.
